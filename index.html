I’m a Molecular Biologist with a Ph.D. in DNA repair and replication stress biology, driven by a deep curiosity about how cells maintain genome integrity and how these processes can be leveraged to improve cancer therapy. My work sits at the intersection of molecular biology and translational research, with a strong focus on generating insights that can eventually make a real difference in patient care.
